I'm a beginner, which guitar should I get?
Welcome to the guitar world! Most beginners tend to get a cheaper guitar so that they can save money for their fav. Because guitars are slightly different in terms of tones that you can differentiate after some practice. It is always the best to buy a basic yet net guitar in the beginning.
What's the best way to learn to play guitar?
Practice and practice a ton!
My acoustic guitar string hurts my finger. What should I do?
Practice a ton until you thicken your finger skin. At that point you will feel nothing. Another option is to buy a better guitar, even though it reduces your pain partially.
